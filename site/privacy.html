Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Overview

ROBO.APP ("Robo") is an open-source iOS app that turns your phone's sensors into structured data for AI agents. We are committed to your privacy.

Data Collection

Robo processes sensor data (LiDAR scans, barcodes, motion data) on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on our servers unless you explicitly choose to export it.

What Stays on Your Device

  • All sensor captures (LiDAR room scans, barcode data, motion readings)
  • Scan history and saved results
  • App preferences and settings

What You Control

  • Export via email/ZIP: You choose when and where to send your data
  • Cloud sync (optional): If enabled, data is sent to our Cloudflare Workers backend. You can delete your data at any time.
  • Device permissions: Camera, LiDAR, and motion access are requested only when needed and can be revoked in iOS Settings

Third-Party Services

Robo does not include any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. When cloud sync is enabled, data is processed via Cloudflare Workers and stored in Cloudflare D1.

Data Security

All network communication uses HTTPS. On-device data is stored using iOS SwiftData with standard iOS data protection.

Children's Privacy

Robo is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
