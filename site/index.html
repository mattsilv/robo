AI agents can plan a room layout, suggest dinner, or pick your outfit — but only if they can see what you see. Robo turns your phone into their eyes. One tap to capture, and it syncs to any agent you choose.

How It Works

1 An AI agent requests data — "I need a floor plan of your bedroom" or "Show me what's in the fridge." The request appears in your Robo inbox.
2 You capture it in one tap — Robo activates the right sensor (LiDAR, camera, barcode) and guides you through a clean capture.
3 It syncs automatically — the data goes straight back to the agent. No uploading, no file juggling. You stay in control of what gets shared.
